Saleshub for Indirect Business— Overview and Key Features.

Over more than a decade, Upject has built the Saleshub Application in close cooperation with Canon. Saleshub's core system features a Configure/Price/Quote solution tailored specifically to Canon's requirements, and over the years it has gained more and more features and modules, making it a powerful toolkit for both, direct and indirect sales. It has since been in use in a number of NSOs including Austria, Germany, Netherlands and Switzerland, and can look back on more than 10 years of battle proven operation. This document outlines the key features within the context of indirect sales functionality.

Key Features

  • Rich and up-to-date product information
    Give your partners direct access to always up-to-date prices, product mappings, service information, product availability, product news, and more.
  • Easy ordering process
    Standard shopping cart orders, backed by promotions, product bundles, special price agreements, etc.
  • Communication Interface between Partner and Canon
    Let your account managers and partners together work on projects, assemble configurations and negotiate project prices.
  • Assisted Configuration
    Avoid configuration mistakes using Saleshub's configuration rules, which ensure valid product combinations.
  • Flexible and customizable Approval Process
    Define a custom mandate structure, based on various project properties, and make sure all required approvals are given.
  • Clean Orders
    All this leading to a clean order, which can be processed by order management and transferred to Oracle without the need of redundant manual checks.

B2B Webshop

Using Saleshub's webshop functionality, partners can place standard orders, just as you would when doing your online shopping. Promotions, product bundles and special price agreements are automatically applied. Recurring orders can be saved and reused, and the progress of the placed orders can be tracked in the application.

Quotes and Configuration

Quotes are the heart of the saleshub application. They cover all aspects of a project, like

  • Hardware configuration of main body, accessories and consumables
  • Software and licences (including automatic SSP calculation)
  • Services, both contract services and installation and setup services
  • Click Service
  • Flatrate Contracts
  • Trad-In and Top-Up
  • Supports

Assisted Configuration

By setting up configuration rules and product dependencies, you can define which products have to go with each other, which products can't be combined, and much more. This avoids configuration mistakes and resulting additional deliveries, and helps your sales team save time and money.

Figures and Profitability

The Saleshub application continuously updates all relevant figures and calculates the project profitability with each change in the configuration. Partners, sales staff and management always have the numbers at hand which they need to make an informed decision.

Project Requests

Partners can enter project requests, requesting target prices for specific projects. Based on a fully adjustable workflow definition, these project requests enter an approval process involving all relevant roles. After a successful approval of the request, partners can order the goods with the approved target price directly in the application.

Depending on project volume, product types, delivery and payment conditions, service contract, etc. the approval process can go through various steps, and might involve sales managers, service departement, finance departement, up to the management board, just to make an example. The approval process is fully customizable and adjustable to your business processes and compliancy rules.

Different Views for different Roles

Saleshub can store various types of prices. Each role is assigned to a specific pricing scheme, this allows you to set salesman cost prices for your sales team, while avoiding to reveal your landed cost prices.

Of course this is only an example, and the pricing structure is completely flexible and can be fully customized.

Document Exports

Both, Canon staff and the Partner can export and download various documents out of a quote, like contracts, spreadsheets and entire proposals, created using Saleshub's built in Proposal Editor.

Dynamic Price Pipeline

Besides storing various imported product prices, Saleshub utilizes a dynamic price pipeline, which allows calculation of dynamic price variations and different pricing schemes for each Partner. Additionally, special price agreements on product level can be set up for each partner individually.

Flexible Partner Setup

Create users with different roles for your Partner, define their product portfolio, create discount schedules and special price conditions. To map your large enterprise partners' complex organisation structures, set up multiple accreditions (Platinum, Gold, Silver, ...) for a single partner.

Rebuild Machines

Use Saleshub's second hand machine database to give your sales staff an overview of available rebuild machines, and let them reserve and offer them to your partners.

Reporting and Data Exploration

Saleshub lets you export various reports from project level down to each single ordered item. Also, Saleshub's Data Explorer lets you browse through the numbers directly in the application.
